Vegan Tikka Masala

Traditional Indian, like Mediterrean, is full of beautiful colors and might literally be where "the spice of life" saying comes from. Also given the even richer history, also another cuisine easily modified.

With the flavor blasting sauces, you can't go wrong no matter what you throw in, the below is just how I like mine!

Let's see what the world has to offer! (Serves 2)

  • teriyaki tofu (1/2 pack)
  • basmati rice (1 cup)
  • medium red onion (1)
  • premade vegan tikka masala sauce
  • vegetable oil (50mL)

Throwing it together, the messier the better!

  1. Heat up vegetable oil in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Cook the basmati rice on medium to high heat for 15 minutes, letting it stea
  3. Slice the tofu into cubes and place into the vegetable oil.
  4. Mince the red onion and hold to the side.
  5. Once the tofu has been in for about 3 minutes, add in the red onions.
  6. When tofu has browned and onions still contain a tougher consistency, put in half of the tikka masala sauce on low heat.
  7. After the tikka masala sauce heats up with the tofu and onions, take off heat.
  8. Plate rice and tikka masala and enjoy!
